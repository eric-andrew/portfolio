Problem

The biggest challenge here is that we need an e-commerce platform that would stop the client from going through with their purchase until they have chosen an event date that is open. There isn't a turnkey solution that handles that business need and comes in under budget.

Solution

I designed a simple and elegant solution with a datepicker flow but, we had to work within Squarespace's e-commerce constraints. Operating on a minimal budget, we decided to use the design shown up minus the datepicker to get the business started.

Takeaways

I completed development and am happy with the outcome and am really glad that we were able to feature the amazing product photography she had done.
