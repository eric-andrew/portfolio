Problem

The ecosystem as SAP is robust and the amount of customization that customers need for their apps would be impossible to handle even for a company as large as this one. There are thousands of applications built for dozens of business needs serviced by one design language.

Solution

The build project aimed to put that customization in the hands of people at every step of the business process by providing them with a prototyping tool that gave them production level code.

Takeaways

I learned so much from this project. It was enormous and had five teams working in four different countries. In addition to working on an agile development team, I was involved in a lot of design thinking, research and UX work as well as a good deal of Project and Product Management.
